# 📈 GTM (Go-To-Market) Playbook: Autotube "Day 1"

As a bootstrapped technical startup, our goal is finding **10 paying customers** who absolutely *love* this product. Until we hit $1,000 MRR, we survive on zero-customer-acquisition-cost (CAC) organic marketing.

## 🎯 The "Bullseye" Target Audience: The Faceless Channel Creator
We aren't creating a platform for Netflix or MrBeast (yet).
Our target is the **20 to 35-year-old Hustler** trying to build a passive income stream through Faceless YouTube Channels (e.g., Psychology facts, stoic quotes, scary stories). 
* **Their Problem:** They are spending $15/hr on Fiverr editors or wasting 8 hours a week manually chaining ChatGPT -> ElevenLabs -> CapCut -> YouTube.
* **Our Solution:** "Connect your channel. Type a niche. Wake up tomorrow with 7 viral Shorts uploaded."

## ⚔️ The Acquisition Strategy

### Tactic 1: Build-in-Public (X / Twitter)
As your technical co-founder, I strongly recommend you launch this as an "Indie Hacker."
* **The Hook:** *"I built an AI that runs my faceless YouTube channels while I sleep. Here's exactly how it chains Gemini 1.5 with Pexels to print passive income."*
* **The Action:** Post architecture diagrams, time-lapses of the Celery worker rendering 5 videos simultaneously, and actual YouTube analytics screenshots of views rolling in from automated uploads.
* **The Call-to-Action:** Reply with "AUTOMATE" and I'll DM you the free beta access link.

### Tactic 2: Niche Reddit Infiltration
Reddit hates self-promotion but loves transparent technical deep-dives.
* Subreddits: `r/SaaS`, `r/SideProject`, `r/YoutubeAutomation`, `r/ChatGPT`
* **The Hook:** *"We spent 3 months reverse-engineering YouTube Shorts retention metrics. We packaged the algorithm into an open-source(ish) AI video generator."*
* **The Action:** Write a massive, insanely valuable post explaining how FFmpeg and LLMs can replace manual video editing. Give away 90% of the secret. Give them the `autotube.vercel.app` link for the last 10% convenience.

### Tactic 3: Product Hunt "Micro-Launch"
Product Hunt brings in free traffic, but only if positioned correctly.
* **Tagline:** Autotube: "The AI Employee for YouTube Automation."
* **Launch Video:** Not a boring voiceover. A split-screen! Left side: A guy drinking coffee. Right side: The Autotube dashboard automatically rendering, scripting, and publishing 3 TikToks in fast-forward. 

## 💰 The Conversion Funnel

Once they land on `autotube.vercel.app`:
1. **The Hook:** "Enter your Niche" (Giant input box on the landing page).
2. **The "Aha" Moment:** Instantly show them 5 generated video titles perfectly tailored to their niche.
3. **The Wall:** "Sign in with Google to generate and upload these videos."
4. **The Upsell:** They get 5 free videos on the Free tier. When they see the magic work, they hit the paywall. At $29/mo (Pro Tier), they're paying $1/day for an infinite video editor. It’s a no-brainer.
